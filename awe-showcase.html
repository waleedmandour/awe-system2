About

What is AWE?

The AWE (Automated Writing Evaluation) system is a multimodal, LLM-based platform designed to provide formative assessment for student writing at the Center for Preparatory Studies, Sultan Qaboos University. It is available in two complementary formats: a full-featured Progressive Web App (PWA) that can be installed on any device for an app-like experience with instant results, and a Google Forms-based system that offers a quick, no-installation submission workflow. Both systems use OCR to extract text from handwritten essays and evaluate the writing against IELTS-aligned rubrics calibrated to CEFR A1–A2 proficiency levels, delivering detailed, actionable assessment reports in seconds.

Process

How It Works

Four simple steps from handwritten essay to detailed AI-powered feedback.

Submit Writing

Students upload photos of their handwritten essays via the Google Form, selecting their course and AI engine preference.

OCR Processing

Google Vision API extracts text from the handwritten scripts with industry-leading handwriting recognition accuracy.

AI Assessment

Gemini or OpenAI evaluates the extracted text against CEFR A1–A2 aligned rubrics across four criteria.

Detailed Feedback

Students receive scores with justifications, error identification with exact quotes, and improvement suggestions.

Features

Assessment Features

A comprehensive suite of AI-powered tools designed for accurate, transparent writing evaluation.

Multi-Engine AI

Choose between Gemini or OpenAI as the assessment engine, allowing comparison and validation of evaluation results.

Google Vision OCR

Industry-leading handwriting recognition technology extracts text from student essays with high accuracy and reliability.

Detailed Rubrics

IELTS-aligned rubrics with score justifications ensure transparent, criterion-referenced evaluation every time.

Error Identification

Exact quotes from the student text are cited alongside explanations, pinpointing specific areas needing improvement.

Actionable Feedback

Specific, level-appropriate suggestions for A1–A2 learners help students understand exactly how to improve their writing.

Instant Results

Real-time assessment delivered in seconds, providing immediate formative feedback to support the learning process.
